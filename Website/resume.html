About Me

I'm a Product Manager and Biomedical Innovator passionate about transforming complex science into human-centered solutions that improve care, access, and outcomes. With over five years of experience spanning medical devices, biotechnology, digital health, and patient experience design, I specialize in building products that merge clinical evidence, design thinking, and market intelligence.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between R&D, policy, and patient reality—whether developing data-driven health dashboards, creating AI frameworks for clinical diagnostics, or leading translational design for wearable therapeutics.

I'm currently advancing this mission through a Healthcare Policy & Compliance Fellowship with NPLB Biotech, where I explore the intersection of biomedical regulation, data ethics, and commercialization strategy.
